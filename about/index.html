WHAT IS JUNCTION?

NO BULLSHIT

Hackathons are all about taking something you love, combining it with tech and learning something in the process. Junction brings together hackers, designers, the best partners and hardware for one weekend of hacking. Charge your laptops and bring your friends for an amazing event. Let's hack!

NOT YOUR ORDINARY LOCATION

Held annually in the cold and slushy Helsinki of late November, our event locations are not of the ordinary sort. In 2015 we were based in an old boiler hall, with lasers and smoke machines coming together to create a crazy venue. Outside, a 1000m2 tent was built by our volunteers from the ground up. This year (2016) we are settling in an old abandoned ship warehouse, where each room was dedicated to creating a part of a ship. You can still see its use in it's structure: long halls filled with smaller private rooms for quiet working.

Junction is Europe’s leading hackathon and embodies the blooming startup ecosystem of the Nordics. We don’t believe that’s by chance. What happens when you combine ambitious hackers from around the world with innovative industry partners and gather them in a relaxed open-minded environment? The innovations of tomorrow are born.

FOR HACKERS, MAKERS AND ENTREPRENEURS

Junction is a convergence point for 1.2k developers, designers, and entrepreneurs from 30+ countries. It's a place where no idea is too crazy, and everyone is welcomed.

You have 48 hours to burn and the latest and greatest hardware and API’s at your fingertips. From drones to thermal cameras, VR Headsets to IOT components, we aim to to bring it all to Junction. Need something specific? Contact us and we'll see what we can do.

Whether you’re a beginner or a pro, if you have the passion and a will to create something, we want you at Junction. There's workshops, talks and tutorials suited for multiple different skill levels.
